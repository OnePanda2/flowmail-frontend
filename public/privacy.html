Privacy Policy for FlowMail Last updated: 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how FlowMail ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, stores, and protects user information when you use the FlowMail Chrome Extension. By installing or using FlowMail, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. FlowMail is designed to help users compose emails more efficiently by providing AI-powered writing assistance. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data is handled responsibly and securely. 1. Information We Collect We collect only the minimum amount of information required to provide the functionality of the extension. Depending on how you use FlowMail, we may collect the following types of information: 1.1 Account Information When you log in to FlowMail, we may collect your email address or login identifier in order to verify your account and subscription status. 1.2 Subscription Information We store information related to your subscription, such as whether you have access to premium features. This information is required to enable or disable paid features. 1.3 Email Content (On Demand Only) FlowMail only accesses email content when you explicitly request AI assistance. The extension does not automatically read, store, or monitor your emails. Email content is processed only for the purpose of generating AI-based suggestions or replies when the user clicks the AI button or triggers the feature manually. 1.4 Technical Data We may collect limited technical data such as extension version, error logs, or usage events in order to improve performance, fix bugs, and maintain service stability. This data does not include personal conversations unless required for the requested AI response. 2. How We Use the Information We use collected information only for the following purposes: * To verify user login and subscription status * To enable premium features * To generate AI-powered email replies when requested by the user * To maintain, improve, and secure the extension * To prevent misuse, fraud, or unauthorized access * To provide support when users contact us We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties. 3. Data Sharing We do not sell, rent, or trade user data. However, limited data may be processed by trusted third-party services strictly for the purpose of providing core functionality, including: * Supabase (for authentication and subscription storage) * Razorpay (for payment processing) * AI API providers (for generating email responses) These services only receive the information necessary to perform their function and are required to handle data securely. 4. Data Storage and Security User data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. Subscription and login data are stored using Supabase secure database services. Payments are handled by Razorpay, and FlowMail does not store full payment details such as card numbers. We take reasonable steps to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system on the internet can guarantee absolute security. 5. User Control and Data Removal Users have full control over their login session. You can sign out at any time using the Sign Out button in the extension popup. When you sign out, stored login information is removed from the browser. If you wish to request deletion of your stored account data, you may contact us using the email provided below. 6. Permissions Used by the Extension FlowMail may request browser permissions required for its functionality, including access to email pages where the extension is used. These permissions are used only to provide AI writing assistance and are not used for tracking or unrelated data collection. 7. Third-Party Services FlowMail relies on third-party services to provide certain features. These services may process data as needed to perform their function. Examples include: * Authentication services * Payment gateways * AI processing APIs * Hosting providers Each third-party provider has its own privacy policy, and users are encouraged to review them. 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the extension, legal requirements, or service providers. Children's Privacy FlowMail is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
